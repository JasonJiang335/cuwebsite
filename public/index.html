Welcome to Chinese Union at University of California, San Diego

加州大学圣地亚哥分校中国同学联合会是UCSD规模最大的华人社团，以服务华人学生社区，弘扬中国文化为宗旨，CU正在以自己独特的影响力，日益受到UCSD校方和华人学生的重视和爱戴。

Welcome Everybody! Chinese Union is a non-profit organization aiming at helping students in both academic and social life in Southern California and encourages cultural interactions between different ethic groups in the community.
